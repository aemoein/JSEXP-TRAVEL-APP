Austin

Austin is the capital city of the state of Texas, located in the southern United States. Known for its live music scene, outdoor activities, and vibrant culture, Austin is a popular destination for visitors and a beloved home for its residents. The city is home to attractions such as the Texas State Capitol, Lady Bird Lake, and the University of Texas at Austin, as well as unique neighborhoods that offer a taste of local flavor.

Top Attractions

Lady Bird Lake

A beautiful lake situated in the heart of Austin, Lady Bird Lake is a popular spot for outdoor activities like kayaking, paddleboarding, and hiking on the trails around the lake. It also offers stunning views of the city skyline and is a great place to relax and enjoy nature.

Barton Springs Pool

A natural swimming pool located in the heart of Austin, Barton Springs is a popular spot for locals and visitors alike. The pool is fed by underground springs and is a refreshing escape from the Texas heat. It also features a beautiful surrounding park for picnics and relaxation.

Texas State Capitol

The iconic Texas State Capitol building is a must-visit attraction for history buffs and architecture enthusiasts. Visitors can take guided tours of the building and learn about the history of Texas politics and government. The building is also surrounded by beautiful grounds perfect for a picnic or a stroll.

South Congress Avenue

A vibrant street lined with unique shops, restaurants, and music venues, South Congress Avenue is a popular destination for visitors looking to experience Austin's unique culture. Visitors can also snap a photo in front of the iconic "I love you so much" mural on the side of Jo's Coffee.

Zilker Park

A large park situated in the heart of Austin, Zilker Park offers a variety of activities for visitors, including hiking and biking trails, a botanical garden, and a mini train ride. It also hosts several annual events, including the Austin City Limits Music Festival.

The University of Texas at Austin

A top-ranked university in the heart of the city, the University of Texas at Austin is a beautiful campus featuring stunning architecture and a vibrant student culture. Visitors can take a self-guided tour of the campus or attend a Longhorns football game at Darrell K Royal-Texas Memorial Stadium.

The Domain

An upscale shopping and entertainment destination, The Domain features a variety of high-end shops, restaurants, and a movie theater. Visitors can also enjoy live music and events in the outdoor plaza area.

Mount Bonnell

A popular spot for hikers and nature lovers, Mount Bonnell offers stunning views of the city skyline and the surrounding hill country. Visitors can hike up to the top of the hill and enjoy a picnic

Hotels & Accommodation

The Beverly Hills Hotel

This iconic hotel has hosted numerous celebrities and offers luxurious accommodations, a spa, and multiple dining options.

The Standard, Downtown LA

This trendy hotel is located in the heart of downtown Los Angeles and offers a rooftop pool, multiple dining options, and a 24-hour fitness center.

Ace Hotel Downtown LA

This boutique hotel features unique decor and a rooftop bar with stunning views of the city skyline.

Getting Around

Metro

The Metro is Los Angeles' rapid transit system, providing access to popular destinations throughout the city. Tickets can be purchased at stations or through the mobile app.

Bike Share

Los Angeles also has a bike share program, with hundreds of bikes available for rent at various stations throughout the city. Users can sign up for a membership or purchase a day pass.

Rental Car

If you prefer to drive, rental cars are available at the airport and throughout the city. Just be prepared for traffic!
