Seattle

Seattle is a bustling city in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States, known for its coffee culture, tech industry, and outdoor recreation opportunities. The city is situated between the Puget Sound and Lake Washington, surrounded by picturesque mountains and forests. Seattle has a vibrant music scene and is home to many famous musicians, including Jimi Hendrix and Nirvana. Visitors can explore the city's many unique neighborhoods, each with its own character and attractions, or venture outside the city to nearby national parks and natural wonders.

Top Attractions

Space Needle

The Space Needle is an iconic observation tower and restaurant that offers stunning views of Seattle's skyline and surrounding scenery. Visitors can take the elevator to the top and enjoy 360-degree views, or dine at the rotating SkyCity restaurant.

Pike Place Market

This historic public market is one of Seattle's most popular attractions. Visitors can explore the market's stalls and shops, watch fishmongers toss fish, and enjoy a variety of local foods and drinks.

Chihuly Garden and Glass

This stunning museum showcases the glass art of Dale Chihuly, a renowned artist from the Pacific Northwest. Visitors can admire the colorful glass sculptures and installations, and learn about Chihuly's creative process.

Museum of Pop Culture

This museum celebrates popular culture and features exhibits on music, science fiction, and more. Visitors can explore interactive exhibits, see rare artifacts, and attend concerts and events.

Food & Drink

Clam Chowder

Seattle is famous for its clam chowder, with some of the best places to try it being Pike Place Chowder, Ivar's Acres of Clams, and Duke's Chowder House.

Coffee

Seattle is known as the coffee capital of the United States, with numerous coffee shops to choose from. Some of the most famous ones include Starbucks, Tully's Coffee, and Seattle's Best Coffee.

Craft Beer

Seattle is also known for its thriving craft beer scene, with some of the best breweries being Fremont Brewing, Pike Brewing Company, and Georgetown Brewing Company.

Hotels & Accommodation

Fairmont Olympic Hotel

This luxurious hotel is located in the heart of downtown Seattle and offers elegant rooms and suites with modern amenities. Amenities include a spa, fitness center, and several dining options.

Ace Hotel Seattle

This trendy boutique hotel is situated in the Belltown neighborhood and offers stylish rooms with vintage-inspired decor. Guests can also enjoy the on-site coffee shop and bar.

Green Tortoise Hostel

This budget-friendly hostel is located in the Pike Place Market area and offers dormitory and private rooms with shared bathrooms. Amenities include a kitchen, lounge, and free breakfast.

Getting Around

Light Rail

The Seattle Light Rail provides quick and easy access to downtown Seattle and the airport. Tickets can be purchased at stations or through the mobile app.

Streetcar

The Seattle Streetcar provides transportation between the South Lake Union and First Hill neighborhoods. Tickets can be purchased at stations or through the mobile app.

Seattle Bike Share

Seattle also has a bike share program, with hundreds of bikes available for rent at various stations throughout the city. Users can sign up for a membership or purchase a day pass.
