United States

The United States is a diverse and vast country, known for its iconic landmarks, diverse cultures, and vibrant cities. With a population of over 331 million people, the United States is one of the largest and most influential countries in the world, with a rich history and varied landscape. Visitors to the United States can experience a wide range of attractions, including national parks, famous cities, historic landmarks, and more.

Quick Facts

Population: 331 million
Capital city: Washington D.C.
Largest city: New York City
Language: English
Currency: United States dollar (USD)
Time Zone: Multiple time zones (UTC−04:00 to UTC−10:00)

Climate

The United States has a varied climate, with different regions experiencing different weather patterns. The northeast and Midwest can experience cold winters and hot summers, while the southern parts of the country are generally warm to hot throughout the year. The west coast has a mild Mediterranean climate, while Alaska and parts of the Rocky Mountains can experience colder temperatures and more extreme weather conditions. Visitors should check the weather in their specific destination before travelling.

Hotels & Accommodation

Fairmont Pacific Rim

This luxurious hotel is located in the heart of downtown Vancouver and offers stunning views of the harbour and mountains. Amenities include a rooftop pool, spa, and multiple dining options.

Loden Hotel

This boutique hotel is situated in the trendy Yaletown neighbourhood and offers stylish rooms and suites with high-end amenities. Guests can also enjoy the on-site fitness centre and spa.

The Burrard

This retro-style hotel is located in the vibrant neighbourhood of downtown Vancouver and offers quirky rooms with mid-century modern decor. Amenities include a courtyard and bike rentals.

Getting Around

SkyTrain

The SkyTrain is Vancouver's rapid transit system, providing quick and convenient access to downtown, the airport, and other key destinations. Tickets can be purchased at stations or through the mobile app.

Bike Share

Vancouver also has a bike share program, with hundreds of bikes available for rent at various stations throughout the city. Users can sign up for a membership or purchase a day pass.

Ferry

For those looking to explore the surrounding areas, ferries are available from Vancouver to destinations like Victoria, the Gulf Islands, and the Sunshine Coast. Reservations are recommended, especially during peak season.
