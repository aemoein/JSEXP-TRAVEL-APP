Boston

Boston is the capital city of the state of Massachusetts, located in the northeastern United States. Known for its rich history, world-class universities, and thriving arts and culture scene, Boston is a popular destination for visitors and a beloved home for its residents. The city is home to attractions such as the Freedom Trail, Fenway Park, and the Boston Common, as well as diverse neighborhoods that offer a taste of local flavor.

Top Attractions

Freedom Trail

Explore 2.5 miles of historical sites in Boston, including 16 iconic landmarks like the Massachusetts State House and the Paul Revere House.

Fenway Park

Visit America's oldest ballpark and home of the Boston Red Sox. Take a tour of the stadium or catch a game to experience the electric atmosphere.

Boston Common

Escape the city hustle and bustle at Boston Common, a 50-acre park in the heart of downtown. Enjoy walking paths, a playground, and seasonal events like ice skating.

Museum of Fine Arts

Discover over 500,000 works of art from around the world at the Museum of Fine Arts. Collections include American, European, and Asian art, as well as special exhibitions.

New England Aquarium

Explore the wonders of the ocean at the New England Aquarium, home to thousands of marine animals including sea turtles, sharks, and penguins. Don't miss the giant ocean tank!

Harvard University

Take a tour of one of the world's most prestigious universities. Visit iconic landmarks like Harvard Yard and the John Harvard statue, and learn about the university's history and culture.

Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum

Discover a unique collection of art and artifacts at the Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum, housed in a stunning Venetian-style palace. Highlights include European paintings, sculptures, and decorative arts.

Boston Tea Party Ships and Museum

Step back in time to 1773 and relive the events leading up to the American Revolution at the Boston Tea Party Ships and Museum. Interactive exhibits and live actors bring history to life.

Getting Around

Metro

The Metro is Los Angeles' rapid transit system, providing access to popular destinations throughout the city. Tickets can be purchased at stations or through the mobile app.

Bike Share

Los Angeles also has a bike share program, with hundreds of bikes available for rent at various stations throughout the city. Users can sign up for a membership or purchase a day pass.

Rental Car

If you prefer to drive, rental cars are available at the airport and throughout the city. Just be prepared for traffic!
