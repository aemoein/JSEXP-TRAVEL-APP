Miami

Miami is a vibrant and bustling city located in the southeastern part of the United States. Known for its beautiful beaches, diverse cultural scene, and warm weather, Miami is a popular destination for tourists and locals alike. Visitors can explore the city's many neighborhoods, each with its own unique character and attractions, or enjoy the delicious cuisine that Miami is famous for.

Quick Facts

Population: 463,347
State: Florida
Time Zone: Eastern Time Zone (UTC-05:00)
Language: English, Spanish
Currency: US Dollar (USD)

Climate

Miami has a tropical monsoon climate with hot and humid summers and warm winters. The average temperature in summer ranges from 25°C (77°F) to 32°C (90°F), while in winter it ranges from 15°C (59°F) to 23°C (73°F). The rainy season in Miami is from May to October, with occasional hurricanes and tropical storms. Visitors are advised to bring light clothing and sunscreen year-round.

Top Attractions

South Beach

Famous for its white sand beaches and pastel-colored Art Deco buildings, South Beach is a must-visit destination in Miami. Visitors can soak up the sun, people-watch on Ocean Drive, and explore the area's shops and restaurants.

Vizcaya Museum and Gardens

This stunning Italian Renaissance-style villa was built in 1916 and features beautiful gardens, fountains, and sculptures. Visitors can take a guided tour of the house and explore the grounds at their leisure.

Everglades National Park

Located just outside Miami, this unique ecosystem is home to a variety of plant and animal species, including alligators, panthers, and manatees. Visitors can take a guided tour of the park, go hiking or kayaking, or take an airboat ride through the wetlands.

Wynwood Walls

This outdoor art gallery features colorful murals and street art by local and international artists. Visitors can explore the area on foot or by bike, and stop at one of the many cafes and restaurants for a bite to eat.

Food & Drink

Cuban Sandwich

The Cuban sandwich is a staple of Miami cuisine, featuring roasted pork, ham, Swiss cheese, pickles, and mustard on Cuban bread. Some of the best places to try a Cuban sandwich in Miami are Enriqueta's Sandwich Shop, Versailles Restaurant, and La Carreta.

Stone Crab

Stone crab claws are a delicacy in Miami, and are usually served with mustard sauce. Some of the best places to try stone crab in Miami are Joe's Stone Crab, Truluck's Seafood, Steak and Crab House, and Garcia's Seafood Grille & Fish Market.

Mojito

The mojito is a refreshing cocktail made with rum, lime, sugar, mint, and soda water. Some of the best places to enjoy a mojito in Miami are Ball & Chain, The Broken Shaker, and Lagniappe.

Hotels & Accommodation

Fontainebleau Miami Beach

This iconic hotel is located on Collins Avenue in Miami Beach and features luxurious rooms and suites, several dining options, a spa, and a pool.

The Biltmore Hotel

This historic hotel is located in Coral Gables and offers elegant rooms and suites, a golf course, a spa, and several dining options.

Freehand Miami

This trendy hostel is located in Miami Beach and offers dormitory and private rooms, a pool, a bar, and a restaurant.

Getting Around

Miami Metrorail

The Metrorail is Miami's rapid transit system, providing quick and easy access to most of the city's attractions. Tickets can be purchased at stations or through the mobile app.

Citi Bike

Citi Bike is Miami's bike share program, with hundreds of bikes available for rent at various stations throughout the city. Users can sign up for the program through the app
