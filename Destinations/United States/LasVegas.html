Las Vegas

Las Vegas is a city located in the southern part of Nevada in the United States. Known for its vibrant nightlife, world-class casinos, and luxury resorts, Las Vegas is a popular destination for visitors from around the world. The city is home to iconic attractions such as the Las Vegas Strip, the Bellagio Fountains, and the Hoover Dam, as well as a wide variety of restaurants, shows, and entertainment venues.

Top Attractions

The Strip

Experience the vibrant energy of Las Vegas by taking a stroll down The Strip. You'll find world-famous hotels, casinos, and entertainment venues, as well as a variety of restaurants, shops, and attractions.

Bellagio Fountains

Witness the stunning water and light show at the Bellagio Fountains, which features choreographed water jets that dance to music and lights. The show is free to the public and takes place every 30 minutes in the evening.

High Roller Observation Wheel

Ride the world's tallest observation wheel, the High Roller, and take in breathtaking views of the Las Vegas skyline and surrounding desert landscape. The wheel is 550 feet tall and takes 30 minutes to complete one rotation.

Fremont Street Experience

Explore the vibrant Fremont Street Experience, a pedestrian mall in downtown Las Vegas that features a variety of shops, restaurants, and entertainment options. Don't miss the nightly light and sound show, Viva Vision.

Hoover Dam

Take a day trip to the Hoover Dam, a marvel of engineering that spans the Colorado River between Arizona and Nevada. Visitors can take guided tours, explore the Visitor Center and Museum, and even walk across the top of the dam.

Neon Museum

Discover the history of Las Vegas through its iconic neon signs at the Neon Museum. This outdoor museum features more than 200 signs from old casinos and businesses, as well as a variety of special exhibitions and events.

Red Rock Canyon

Escape the city and explore the natural beauty of Red Rock Canyon, located just a short drive from Las Vegas. Visitors can hike, bike, or drive through the stunning desert landscape, and even see ancient petroglyphs and fossils.

The Mob Museum

Learn about the history of organized crime in America at The Mob Museum, located in downtown Las Vegas. The museum features interactive exhibits, artifacts, and immersive experiences that bring the stories of the mob to life.

Getting Around

Metro

The Metro is Los Angeles' rapid transit system, providing access to popular destinations throughout the city. Tickets can be purchased at stations or through the mobile app.

Bike Share

Los Angeles also has a bike share program, with hundreds of bikes available for rent at various stations throughout the city. Users can sign up for a membership or purchase a day pass.

Rental Car

If you prefer to drive, rental cars are available at the airport and throughout the city. Just be prepared for traffic!
