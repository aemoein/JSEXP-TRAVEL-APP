Phuket

Phuket is a beautiful island located in the south of Thailand. Known for its stunning beaches, crystal clear waters, and delicious cuisine, it is a popular destination for tourists from all over the world. Visitors can enjoy a variety of activities, from relaxing on the beach to exploring the island's natural beauty and cultural heritage. The city is also home to a bustling nightlife scene, with numerous bars, nightclubs, and restaurants to choose from.

Quick Facts

Population: 416,582
Province: Phuket
Time Zone: Indochina Time (UTC+07:00)
Language: Thai
Currency: Thai baht (THB)

Climate

Phuket has a tropical monsoon climate with high temperatures and high humidity year-round. The rainy season lasts from May to October, with the heaviest rainfall in September and October. The dry season runs from November to April, with the hottest months being March and April. Visitors should pack light, comfortable clothing and be prepared for occasional rain showers.

Top Attractions

Beaches

Phuket is renowned for its beautiful beaches, which offer crystal-clear waters, white sand, and stunning views. Some of the top beaches to visit include Patong Beach, Karon Beach, and Kata Beach.

Big Buddha

This 45-meter-tall statue of Buddha is one of the most popular attractions in Phuket. Visitors can climb the stairs to the top of the statue and enjoy panoramic views of the island.

Phang Nga Bay

This stunning bay is known for its limestone cliffs, emerald-green waters, and picturesque islands. Visitors can take a boat tour of the bay and explore its many caves, lagoons, and secluded beaches.

Old Phuket Town

This historic neighborhood is filled with charming Sino-Portuguese architecture, colorful shophouses, and street art. Visitors can explore its many boutiques, cafes, and galleries, and learn about the island's rich cultural heritage.

Food & Drink

Sushi

Vancouver is known for its fresh seafood and sushi is a popular choice for locals and visitors alike. Some top spots to try include Miku, Tojo's, and Sushi Bar Maumi.

Craft Beer

Vancouver is also home to a thriving craft beer scene, with a variety of local breweries and taprooms. Some popular options include Brassneck Brewery, Main Street Brewing, and Strange Fellows Brewing.

Poutine

This quintessential Canadian dish consists of French fries, cheese curds, and gravy. Some top spots to try include Fritz European Fry House, La Belle Patate, and Smoke's Poutinerie.

Hotels & Accommodation

Fairmont Pacific Rim

This luxurious hotel is located in the heart of downtown Vancouver and offers stunning views of the harbour and mountains. Amenities include a rooftop pool, spa, and multiple dining options.

Loden Hotel

This boutique hotel is situated in the trendy Yaletown neighbourhood and offers stylish rooms and suites with high-end amenities. Guests can also enjoy the on-site fitness centre and spa.

The Burrard

This retro-style hotel is located in the vibrant neighbourhood of downtown Vancouver and offers quirky rooms with mid-century modern decor. Amenities include a courtyard and bike rentals.

Getting Around

SkyTrain

The SkyTrain is Vancouver's rapid transit system, providing quick and convenient access to downtown, the airport, and other key destinations. Tickets can be purchased at stations or through the mobile app.

Bike Share

Vancouver also has a bike share program, with hundreds of bikes available for rent at various stations throughout the city. Users can sign up for a membership or purchase a day pass.

Ferry

For those looking to explore the surrounding areas, ferries are available from Vancouver to destinations like Victoria, the Gulf Islands, and the Sunshine Coast. Reservations are recommended, especially during peak season.
