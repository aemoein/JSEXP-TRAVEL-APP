Milan

Milan is a fashionable city located in the northern region of Italy. It is the second-most populous city in Italy and is known as the capital of fashion and design. Milan is home to numerous historical landmarks and museums, including the famous Milan Cathedral, the Sforza Castle, and the famous opera house, La Scala. Milan is also famous for its food and wine culture, which includes traditional Italian dishes such as risotto, polenta, and osso buco.

Top Attractions

Duomo di Milano

The Milan Cathedral is one of the largest churches in the world, and a symbol of Milan. Visitors can climb to the roof for panoramic views of the city, or explore the ornate interior with its stained glass windows and intricate sculptures.

Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II

This stunning 19th century shopping arcade is one of the world's oldest and most beautiful. Visitors can shop for designer brands, dine at elegant cafes, or simply admire the intricate architecture and stunning glass roof.

Sforza Castle

This medieval castle in the heart of Milan houses several museums and art collections, including works by Michelangelo and Leonardo da Vinci. Visitors can explore the castle's courtyards and gardens, or admire the impressive collection of armor and weaponry.

Navigli Canals

This historic network of canals in Milan's trendy Navigli district is lined with colorful buildings, cafes, and shops. Visitors can take a boat tour, stroll along the canal walkways, or enjoy an evening drink at one of the many waterfront bars.

Food & Drink

Pizza

Pizza is a classic Italian dish, and Milan has some of the best pizzerias in the country. Some popular options include Piz, Dry Milano, and Gino Sorbillo.

Gelato

Gelato is a must-try dessert in Milan, and you'll find many artisanal gelaterias throughout the city. Some favorites include Il Massimo del Gelato, Grom, and Cioccolati Italiani.

Aperitivo

Aperitivo is a pre-dinner drink and snack that's popular in Milan. Head to Navigli, the city's canal district, for some of the best aperitivo bars in town, such as Rita & Cocktails, Deseo, and Ugo.

Hotels & Accommodation

Bulgari Hotel Milano

This luxurious hotel is located in the heart of Milan's fashion district and offers spacious rooms and suites with elegant decor. Amenities include a spa, rooftop terrace, and gourmet restaurant.

Room Mate Giulia

This chic hotel is situated in the city center and features stylish rooms and suites with modern amenities. Guests can also enjoy the on-site bar and lounge.

Ostello Bello

This trendy hostel offers dormitory and private rooms with shared bathrooms. Amenities include a rooftop terrace, bar, and communal kitchen.

Getting Around

Metro

Milan's metro system provides easy access to most of the city's attractions. Tickets can be purchased at stations or through the mobile app.

Bike Sharing

Bike sharing is a great way to get around Milan, and there are several companies that offer rental bikes throughout the city, such as Mobike, Ofo, and Jump.
