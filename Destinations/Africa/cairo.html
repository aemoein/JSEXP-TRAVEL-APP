Climate

Vancouver has a mild, temperate climate with moderate rainfall throughout the year. Average temperatures range from 2°C (36°F) in winter to 19°C (66°F) in summer, with plenty of sunshine and opportunities for outdoor activities year-round. Remember to pack layers for changing weather conditions.

Top Attractions

Giza Pyramids

The Great Pyramid of Giza is one of the most recognizable structures in the world and the last surviving member of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World. Visitors can also explore the surrounding pyramids, Sphinx, and ancient tombs.

Khan El-Khalili

This bustling bazaar in Islamic Cairo dates back to the 14th century and is a popular destination for shopping and people watching. Visitors can browse for souvenirs, sip tea at a café, or take a walking tour of the area.

Egyptian Museum

Home to one of the world's most extensive collections of ancient Egyptian artifacts, the Egyptian Museum in Cairo is a must-visit for history buffs. Highlights include the Tutankhamun exhibit and the mummy room.

Cairo Citadel

Perched atop a hill in Islamic Cairo, the Cairo Citadel is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a prime example of Islamic military architecture. Visitors can explore the fortifications, mosques, and museums within the citadel walls.

Food & Drink

Koshari

Koshari is a popular Egyptian dish made with rice, lentils, and pasta, topped with a spicy tomato sauce and crispy fried onions. You can try it at El Tahrir Koshari in Downtown Cairo or at Abu Tarek in Midan Tahrir.

Foul

Foul is a hearty and filling breakfast dish made with fava beans, served with bread and optional toppings like tahini, onions, and tomatoes. You can try it at Foul El Gamal in Islamic Cairo or at Foul El Mahshi in Zamalek.

Karkadeh

Karkadeh is a refreshing hibiscus tea that's popular in Egypt. You can find it at many cafes and restaurants throughout Cairo, or try making it yourself with dried hibiscus flowers.

Hotels & Accommodation

Four Seasons Hotel Cairo at Nile Plaza

This luxurious hotel is located on the banks of the Nile River and offers stunning views of the city. Amenities include an outdoor pool, spa, and multiple dining options.

Kempinski Nile Hotel Cairo

This five-star hotel is situated in the Garden City district and offers spacious rooms and suites with modern amenities. Guests can also enjoy the on-site spa, fitness center, and several dining options.

Cairo Marriott Hotel & Omar Khayyam Casino

This historic hotel is located in Zamalek and offers elegant rooms and suites with views of the Nile or the hotel's gardens. Amenities include a casino, spa, and several restaurants.

Getting Around

Metro

The Cairo Metro is the fastest and most convenient way to get around the city. It has three lines that connect many of the major attractions, and tickets can be purchased at stations.

Uber

Uber is available in Cairo and can be ordered directly from the app
