Vancouver

Vancouver is a vibrant and diverse city located on the west coast of Canada. Known for its stunning natural beauty, Vancouver is surrounded by mountains, forests, and beaches, offering endless opportunities for outdoor activities. The city is also home to a thriving arts and culture scene, with world-class museums, galleries, and theaters. Visitors can explore the city's diverse neighborhoods, each with its own unique character and charm, or enjoy the delicious cuisine that Vancouver is famous for. Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, or a little bit of both, Vancouver has something to offer everyone.

Attractions

Etosha National Park

One of the largest game reserves in Africa, Etosha National Park is home to a wide variety of wildlife including lions, elephants, and rhinos.

Sossusvlei

Famous for its towering red sand dunes, Sossusvlei is a must-see destination for nature lovers and photographers.

Fish River Canyon

The second largest canyon in the world, Fish River Canyon offers breathtaking views and hiking trails for adventurous visitors.

Food & Drink

Sushi

Vancouver is known for its fresh seafood and sushi is a popular choice for locals and visitors alike. Some top spots to try include Miku, Tojo's, and Sushi Bar Maumi.

Craft Beer

Vancouver is also home to a thriving craft beer scene, with a variety of local breweries and taprooms. Some popular options include Brassneck Brewery, Main Street Brewing, and Strange Fellows Brewing.

Poutine

This quintessential Canadian dish consists of French fries, cheese curds, and gravy. Some top spots to try include Fritz European Fry House, La Belle Patate, and Smoke's Poutinerie.

Hotels & Accommodation

The Olive Exclusive

This boutique hotel in Windhoek offers luxurious suites, a gourmet restaurant, and a rooftop pool with stunning city views.

Mowani Mountain Camp

Nestled among the boulders of Damaraland, Mowani Mountain Camp offers unique tented accommodations and panoramic views of the desert landscape.

Sossusvlei Lodge

Located just outside the entrance to Sossusvlei, this lodge offers comfortable accommodations, a swimming pool, and guided tours of the area.
