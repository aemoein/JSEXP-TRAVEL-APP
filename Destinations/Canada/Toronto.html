Toronto

Toronto is a diverse and vibrant city located on the shores of Lake Ontario in Canada. Known for its friendly people, vibrant neighborhoods, and beautiful green spaces, Toronto is a popular destination for travelers from all over the world. Visitors can explore the city's unique blend of cultures and traditions by visiting its many museums, galleries, and theaters, or by sampling its world-class cuisine. Toronto is also a hub for business and technology, with a thriving startup scene and a strong economy. Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, or inspiration, Toronto has something for everyone.

Top Attractions

CN Tower

Rising 553 meters above the city, the CN Tower is one of Toronto's most iconic landmarks. Visitors can take an elevator to the observation deck for stunning views, or even try the EdgeWalk, a hands-free walk around the edge of the tower's main pod.

Royal Ontario Museum

The ROM is Canada's largest museum and home to over six million artifacts, artworks, and specimens. Highlights include exhibits on dinosaurs, ancient Egypt, and Canada's First Peoples.

Distillery District

This pedestrian-only neighbourhood is home to over 40 heritage buildings that once housed a large whiskey distillery. Today, the district is a popular spot for dining, shopping, and entertainment.

Toronto Islands

A short ferry ride from downtown Toronto, the Toronto Islands offer a peaceful escape from the city. Visitors can bike, hike, or paddle around the islands, or simply relax on the beach.

Food & Drink

Poutine

Poutine is a Canadian dish that originated in Quebec and consists of fries, cheese curds, and gravy. Some popular places to try poutine in Toronto include Smoke's Poutinerie, Poutini's House of Poutine, and The Fry's Guys.

Peameal Bacon Sandwich

Another Canadian dish that's popular in Toronto is the peameal bacon sandwich. Some great places to try this sandwich include Carousel Bakery at the St. Lawrence Market, Paddington's Pump, and The Senator Restaurant.

Craft Beer

Toronto has a vibrant craft beer scene, with plenty of breweries and brewpubs to choose from. Some notable places to try craft beer in Toronto include Bellwoods Brewery, Blood Brothers Brewing, and Left Field Brewery.

Hotels & Accommodation

Fairmont Royal York

This iconic hotel is located in the heart of downtown Toronto and offers luxurious rooms and suites with modern amenities. Amenities include a spa, fitness center, and several dining options.

The Drake Hotel

This boutique hotel is situated in the trendy West Queen West neighborhood and offers stylish rooms and suites with unique decor. Guests can also enjoy the on-site restaurant, rooftop patio, and live music events.

HI Toronto Hostel

This budget-friendly hostel is located in the Annex neighborhood and offers dormitory and private rooms with shared bathrooms. Amenities include a kitchen, lounge, and rooftop terrace with views of the city.

Getting Around

Subway

Toronto's subway system provides quick and easy access to most of the city's attractions. Tickets can be purchased at stations or through the mobile app.

Bike Share

Toronto has a bike share program called Bike Share Toronto, with hundreds of bikes available for rent at various stations throughout the city. Users can sign up for a membership or purchase a day pass.
