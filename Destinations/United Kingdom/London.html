London

London is the capital and largest city of England and the United Kingdom. It is a global hub of culture, arts, finance, education, and entertainment, attracting millions of visitors every year. The city has a rich history, with landmarks such as the Tower of London, Big Ben, and Buckingham Palace. London is also known for its diverse food scene, with a range of international cuisines and traditional British dishes. Whether you're interested in exploring the city's history, museums, and galleries, or shopping in its famous markets and luxury stores, London has something to offer everyone.

Top Attractions

Coca-Cola London Eye

The Coca-Cola London Eye is a giant Ferris wheel situated on the South Bank of the River Thames. It offers stunning views of London and is one of the most popular tourist attractions in the city.

British Museum

The British Museum is a world-renowned museum that houses a vast collection of art and artifacts from around the world, including the Rosetta Stone and the Parthenon sculptures.

Tower Bridge

Tower Bridge is an iconic symbol of London that spans the River Thames. Visitors can take a tour of the bridge's engine rooms and high-level walkways, and enjoy panoramic views of the city from the top.

Tower of London

The Tower of London is a historic fortress that has served as a royal palace, prison, and armory over the centuries. Visitors can see the Crown Jewels, explore the castle's walls and towers, and learn about its gruesome history.

Food & Drink

Fish and Chips

Fish and chips is a classic British dish and a must-try when in London. Some of the best places to try it are The Golden Hind, Poppies Fish and Chips, and The Sea Shell of Lisson Grove.

Afternoon Tea

Afternoon tea is a traditional British pastime that's become popular around the world. Some of the best places to enjoy it in London are The Ritz, Fortnum & Mason, and The Wolseley.

Gin and Tonic

Gin and tonic is a popular cocktail in London, with many bars and pubs offering their own unique twists on the classic drink. Some of the best places to enjoy a G&T are The Gin Bar at Holborn Dining Room, Mr Fogg's Gin Parlour, and Graphic Bar.

Hotels & Accommodation

The Savoy Hotel

This iconic hotel is located in the heart of London and offers luxurious rooms and suites with stunning views of the River Thames. Amenities include several restaurants and bars, a spa, and a fitness center.

The Ned

This stylish hotel is housed in a historic building and offers rooms and suites with vintage-inspired decor. Guests can also enjoy several restaurants and bars, a rooftop terrace, and a spa.

YHA London St Pancras

This budget-friendly hostel is located near several major transport hubs and offers dormitory and private rooms with shared bathrooms. Amenities include a bar, a communal kitchen, and a game room.

Getting Around

London Underground

The London Underground, also known as the Tube, is the city's rapid transit system, providing quick and easy access to most of the city's attractions. Tickets can be purchased at stations or through the mobile app.

Boris Bikes

Boris Bikes, officially known as Santander Cycles, are London's bike sharing system For those looking to explore the surrounding areas.
