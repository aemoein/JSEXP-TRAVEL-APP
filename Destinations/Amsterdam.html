Vancouver

Vancouver is a vibrant and diverse city located on the west coast of Canada. Known for its stunning natural beauty, Vancouver is surrounded by mountains, forests, and beaches, offering endless opportunities for outdoor activities. The city is also home to a thriving arts and culture scene, with world-class museums, galleries, and theaters. Visitors can explore the city's diverse neighborhoods, each with its own unique character and charm, or enjoy the delicious cuisine that Vancouver is famous for. Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, or a little bit of both, Vancouver has something to offer everyone.

Top Attractions

Stanley Park

One of the largest urban parks in North America, Stanley Park is a lush oasis in the heart of Vancouver. Visitors can explore the park's forests, beaches, and scenic seawall, and check out attractions like the Vancouver Aquarium and the totem poles.

Capilano Suspension Bridge

This iconic suspension bridge spans 140 meters across the Capilano River Canyon and offers stunning views of the surrounding forest. Visitors can also check out the treetop adventure course, cliffwalk, and other attractions at the park.

Grouse Mountain

Grouse Mountain is a popular year-round destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Visitors can take a scenic gondola ride to the mountaintop and enjoy activities like skiing, snowboarding, hiking, ziplining, and wildlife encounters.

Granville Island

This vibrant neighbourhood is home to a bustling public market, artisan studios, and a variety of shops and restaurants. Visitors can also take a boat tour, watch street performers, or check out a show at one of the island's theatres.

Food & Drink

Stroopwafel

A stroopwafel is a Dutch treat made from two thin waffles with a caramel filling. Some top spots to try include Lanskroon, Van Wonderen Stroopwafels, and Original Stroopwafels stand at Albert Cuyp Market.

Poffertjes

Poffertjes are small, fluffy pancakes typically served with butter and powdered sugar. Some popular places to try them include Pancakes Amsterdam, The Pancake Bakery, and De Vier Pilaren.

Jenever

Jenever is a traditional Dutch gin-like liquor. Some great places to try it include Wynand Fockink, Proeflokaal A. van Wees, and De Drie Fleschjes.

Hotels & Accommodation

Amsterdam Marriott Hotel

This luxurious hotel is located in the heart of Amsterdam and offers spacious rooms and suites, a fitness center, and multiple dining options.

Hotel Pulitzer Amsterdam

This boutique hotel is situated in the historic Canal District and offers unique rooms and suites, a garden terrace, and a restaurant and bar.

Lloyd Hotel

This design hotel is located in a historic building and offers rooms and suites with eclectic decor, a restaurant, and a bar.

Getting Around

Bike

Biking is a popular way to get around Amsterdam, with bike rental shops located throughout the city. Some top places to rent a bike include MacBike, Starbikes Rental, and Yellow Bike.

Tram

Amsterdam's tram system is an efficient way to get around the city. Tickets can be purchased at stations or through the GVB app.

Boat

Exploring Amsterdam's canals by boat is a unique and scenic way to see the amazing attractions that Amsterdam has to offer
