Dubai

Dubai is a city in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) known for its luxurious and modern lifestyle, as well as its unique blend of traditional and contemporary cultures. Located on the southeast coast of the Persian Gulf, Dubai is one of the world's most popular tourist destinations, offering a wide range of attractions and activities for visitors of all ages.

From towering skyscrapers and world-class shopping malls to vast deserts and beautiful beaches, Dubai has something to offer everyone. Visitors can experience the thrill of indoor skiing or skydiving, explore the city's historical and cultural landmarks, or indulge in delicious international cuisine. Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, or a little bit of both, Dubai is a truly unique destination that should not be missed.

Quick Facts

Population: 3.4 million
Country: United Arab Emirates
Time Zone: Gulf Standard Time (UTC+04:00)
Language: Arabic, English
Currency: UAE dirham (AED)

Climate

Dubai has a hot desert climate with very little rainfall throughout the year. Summers are extremely hot and humid, with temperatures often reaching above 40°C (104°F), while winters are mild with temperatures ranging from 14°C (57°F) to 23°C (73°F). Visitors should take care to protect themselves from the sun and heat during their stay in Dubai.

Top Attractions

Burj Khalifa

The tallest building in the world, Burj Khalifa offers breathtaking views of Dubai from its observation decks. Visitors can also enjoy dining and shopping in the tower.

Dubai Fountain

This iconic fountain show is set on the Burj Khalifa Lake and features a stunning display of water, music, and lights. The show is free to watch and takes place several times a day.

Dubai Mall

One of the largest shopping malls in the world, Dubai Mall offers a wide range of retail, dining, and entertainment options. Visitors can also check out the indoor aquarium and ice rink.

Palm Jumeirah

This man-made island in the shape of a palm tree offers luxurious hotels, villas, and apartments. Visitors can also enjoy beach activities, water sports, and fine dining.

Food & Drink

Shawarma

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern street food that's available all over Dubai. Some of the best places to try shawarma in Dubai are Al Mallah, Operation: Falafel, and Zaatar w Zeit.

Machboos

Machboos is a traditional Emirati rice dish that's typically made with chicken or lamb. Some of the best places to try machboos in Dubai are Al Fanar Restaurant & Cafe, Seven Sands, and Aseelah.

Karak Tea

Karak tea is a sweet and milky tea that's popular in the Middle East. Some of the best places to enjoy karak tea in Dubai are Tea Junction, Filli Cafe, and Al Ustad Special Kabab.

Hotels & Accommodation

Burj Al Arab

The Burj Al Arab is a luxurious hotel located on a man-made island off the coast of Dubai. It offers stunning views of the Arabian Gulf and features an opulent interior with a gold-plated atrium, a spa, and several dining options.

Atlantis, The Palm

Atlantis, The Palm is a massive resort that's situated on the Palm Jumeirah, a man-made island shaped like a palm tree. It features a water park, aquarium, spa, and multiple restaurants.

Rove Downtown Dubai

Rove Downtown Dubai is a modern and affordable hotel located in the heart of the city. It offers stylish rooms with free Wi-Fi, a 24-hour gym, and a rooftop pool.

Getting Around

Dubai Metro

The Dubai Metro is a rapid transit system that serves the city of Dubai. It's fully automated and offers air-conditioned trains with free Wi-Fi. Tickets can be purchased at stations or through the mobile app.

Dubai Bus

Dubai Bus is a convenient way to explore dubai without getting lost
